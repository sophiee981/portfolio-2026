AI-powered video generation platform built on BNB Chain — turning static images and voice inputs into fully rendered videos. Designed the end-to-end creation flow from asset upload and voice synthesis to on-chain video minting and distribution.

## Scope

- Video creation pipeline: image upload, voice synthesis, AI generation, and preview
- On-chain minting flow and discovery feed on BNB Chain
- Creator dashboard with generation history and earnings tracking

## Impact

- AI video creation simplified to a 3-step flow
- On-chain ownership enabling creators to monetize outputs natively on BNB Chain
